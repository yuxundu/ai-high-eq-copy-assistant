# AI High EQ Copy Assistant – Market Positioning

## Product Overview
AI High EQ Copy Assistant is a lightweight web-based tool designed to help Chinese users craft high‑emotional‑quotient (EQ) messages and copy. It leverages AI to generate polished, empathetic and context‑appropriate text for everyday social interactions.

## Target Audience
- **Primary users:** Chinese‑speaking individuals aged 20–35 who are active on social media and messaging apps.
- **Secondary users:** Professionals who need polished communication (emails, apologies, requests) and online content creators who want engaging comments or captions.

## Market Need
Many people struggle with expressing themselves in a way that feels natural, considerate and engaging. They want to:
- Avoid awkward or blunt messages.
- Craft thoughtful replies, greetings or compliments.
- Save time when writing social posts, comments or short communications.

The product addresses these pain points by delivering ready‑to‑use high‑EQ phrases that fit the context.

## Key Use Cases
- **Personal relationships:** Generate messages to comfort friends, apologize to partners, or express appreciation.
- **Social interactions:** Create witty or heartfelt comments and captions for WeChat Moments, Xiaohongshu, video comments or group chats.
- **Work communications:** Produce polite and respectful emails, requests or updates for coworkers or supervisors.
- **Festivals & events:** Provide seasonal greetings and holiday wishes that feel genuine rather than generic.

## Differentiation & Value Proposition
- **Localized Chinese tone:** Tailored prompts ensure the outputs align with cultural norms and language nuances.
- **Multiple styles:** Users can choose between playful, formal, poetic or humorous styles.
- **Instant results:** A simple interface delivers several options quickly, reducing the time spent brainstorming.
- **Free tier with premium upsell:** The base version offers limited generations; an affordable upgrade unlocks unlimited use and additional templates.

## Revenue Model
- Freemium model with a one‑time ¥9.9 upgrade for unlimited access and extra templates.
- Optional custom message service (¥19.9 per request) where users can ask for bespoke responses.
- Future expansion to subscription bundles that include holiday packs or additional AI‑assisted writing tools.

## Promotion Channels
- **Content marketing:** Share daily examples of high‑EQ messages on Xiaohongshu, Douyin and WeChat Moments to demonstrate effectiveness.
- **Word‑of‑mouth:** Encourage users to share their generated messages with a small signature (“via AI 高情商文案助手”) to drive viral growth.
- **Short videos:** Produce quick tutorials showing before‑and‑after message transformations.
- **Communities:** Engage relevant WeChat groups and forums where people discuss relationships, workplace communication or social media growth.

## Conclusion
By focusing on the universal desire to communicate thoughtfully, AI High EQ Copy Assistant serves a clear and underserved market. Its localized Chinese focus, simple interface and low price point make it a compelling tool for everyday users seeking to improve their social and professional interactions.
